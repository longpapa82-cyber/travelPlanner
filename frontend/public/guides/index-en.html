Travel Preparation Checklist

Before heading abroad, there are essential things to check. First, verify your passport's validity — most countries require at least 6 months of remaining validity upon entry. Depending on your destination, you may need a visa, so research requirements early. Travel insurance is highly recommended to cover unexpected situations like medical emergencies, trip cancellations, or lost luggage. For currency, exchange a small amount before departure and use local ATMs or exchange offices for better rates.

Book flights at least 2-3 months in advance for the best prices. Choose accommodation based on proximity to public transportation and city center access to save time and money on getting around. Prepare a local SIM card or pocket WiFi before departure so you can use maps and translation apps immediately upon arrival.
